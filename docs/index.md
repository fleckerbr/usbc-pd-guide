# Introduction

The USB-C Specification has been extended multiple times since its introduction and it can be difficult to piece together how the various specifications fit together.
The objective of this guide is to walk through the parts of the [USB TC], [USB TCPCI], and [USB PD] Specifications that are relevant to the creation of a USB Type-C Port Manager (TCPM).

!!! note "Terminology Variations"
    Although this guide will generally aim to keep its terminology consistent with the official USB-C specifications,
    there will be cases where naming is altered for the sake of consistency within this guide.
